What We Strip

Three types of work. All handled at our Lakeville facility — no third parties, no shipping.

2-Day Turnaround

Hook Cleaning

Paint buildup on hooks degrades your ground connection — and a weak ground means orange peel, fisheye, and rejects. We strip hooks clean so your line keeps moving and your finish quality stays consistent. Better hooks, better bonds, better coats.

5-Day Turnaround

Rack & Fixture Maintenance

When the buildup on your batch cards, grates, and load bars gets thick enough to matter, bring them in. Burn-off ovens sized for full-scale fixtures. You know when it's time — we make it fast and easy when it is.

2 Days – 1 Week · Expedite Available

Parts Stripping

Rework happens. Spec changes, rejects, parts pulled from service — we strip steel and aluminum down to bare metal, clean and ready for recoating. Chemical process available for aluminum and heat-sensitive parts. Need it faster? Just ask at drop-off.
