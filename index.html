Common Questions

What coatings can you strip?

Powder coat, e-coat, liquid and wet paint, epoxy, enamel, urethane, and most high-temperature industrial coatings. If you're not sure, call us — we'll let you know.

Can you strip aluminum parts?

Yes. Aluminum goes through our chemical stripping process rather than the burn-off oven — burn-off temperatures can damage aluminum. The chemical process is safe for aluminum and most non-ferrous metals. Same result: bare metal, ready for recoating.

How does burn-off work?

Parts go into a thermal oven and are heated to a controlled high temperature. The organic coating oxidizes and burns off, leaving only inorganic ash. The ash is removed, and parts come out clean and ready for recoating. No harsh chemicals, no water discharge.

What's your turnaround time?

Hook cleaning: 2 days standard. Rack and fixture jobs: approximately 5 days. Parts stripping: 2 days to 1 week depending on volume and method. Expedite is available on parts — just ask when you drop off.

What parts and products do you handle?

Hooks, hangers, racks, fixtures, grates, batch cards, load bars, jigs, and reject parts. If you have something outside that list, call us — we're happy to take a look.

How far do customers come from?

Most of our customers are in the Twin Cities metro, but we regularly serve customers from across Minnesota, Wisconsin, Iowa, North Dakota, and South Dakota. Easy semi access at our Lakeville dock makes the trip straightforward. Transportation is available upon request for customers with recurring service relationships — or ship your parts directly to us.
